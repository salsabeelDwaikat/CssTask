Job Description

We are seeking an experienced Fullstack Developer to join our team. As a Fullstack Developer at Manage, you will be responsible for developing and maintaining both the frontend and backend of our platform. You will collaborate with various teams to create seamless and high-quality features for our users.

Responsibilities

  • Develop and maintain both frontend and backend components
  • Collaborate with the team to define and design new features
  • Write clean, efficient, and reusable code
  • Optimize applications for maximum performance and scalability
  • Ensure the technical feasibility of UI/UX designs

Requirements

  • 3+ years of experience as a Fullstack Developer
  • Strong proficiency in Python and React
  • Experience with databases and version control systems
  • Strong problem-solving skills and attention to detail
  • Excellent communication skills
