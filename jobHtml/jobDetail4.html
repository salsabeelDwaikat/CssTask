MyHome is seeking a motivated Junior Frontend Developer to join our growing team. You will help develop and enhance user-facing features, ensuring a seamless and engaging experience for our users.

  • Understanding of responsive design and cross-browser compatibility
  • Strong problem-solving skills and attention to detail
